Donate


"Your contributions to the Organization of Young Filipino Americans at UVA are greatly appreciated. They could benefit our extensive calendar of consistent event programming, our annual excellence scholarship, and especially our major cultural showcases like Barrio Fiesta, Filipino Food Night, and more that encourage celebration of Filipino culture and identity across UVA and the Charlottesville community. If you have additional questions about how your donations could be used, please reach out to the current Treasurer for more information. Thank you."

- Taylor Mendoza, Treasurer 2020-2021


Contact information for the current treasurer can be found in the "B&C" tab at the top.

If you would like to contribute to O.Y.F.A. @ UVA, please click the button below. You will be linked to the PayPal account owned by Owen Haines, former Treasurer of O.Y.F.A., who remains the point of contact for donations.
Thank you!
